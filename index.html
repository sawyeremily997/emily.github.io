About Me: Emily Sawyer

Bio

My name is Emily Sawyer and I'm from Pflugerville, Texas. I came to Texas A&M University in Fall 2020 as a General Engineering major and soon realized that engineering was not my passion. I changed my major to Visualization in Summer 2021 and have never been happier. In addition to my B.S. in Visualization, I am also pursuing minors in both Business and Mathematics.

My hobbies include any and all arts/crafts, but lately I've been knitting, crocheting, and graphic design. I love to go out and explore, especially antique shops. I like to participate in campus traditions so I go to all the home football games, silver taps, bonfire memorial, and muster. Going out is fine, but I also love staying in with a good book or watching a show/movie in bed.
